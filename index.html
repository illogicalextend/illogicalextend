    About

    Hi, I'm Judy Zhao,

    Certified Practising Speech Pathologist

    I am an experienced Speech Pathologist who has worked in a number of different settings in Sydney and London (UK), including servicing NDIS participants in the community (home, childcare centres, and schools), private practice clinics, not-for-profit organisations, and community health centres.

    ​​

    I primarily work with children and young adults in the areas of speech, language, literacy, and social skills. I am passionate about working in partnership with families to ensure that therapy focuses on their priorities, and also builds their skills in supporting their child's communication development.
